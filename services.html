SCAFFOLDING SERVICES IN SURREY & LONDON

From single-home residential projects to large commercial developments — MAG Group Scaffolding has the equipment, team and experience to handle every job safely and on time. Serving Leatherhead, Epsom, Kingston, Guildford, Dorking, Wimbledon, Cobham, Woking, Reigate and the surrounding areas.

01 — RESIDENTIAL

RESIDENTIAL SCAFFOLDING

We provide safe, efficient scaffolding for homeowners across Surrey and London. Whether it's roof maintenance, chimney repairs or a full loft conversion, our residential scaffold is erected quickly and dismantled cleanly.

  • Roof repairs and replacements
  • Loft conversions and dormer builds
  • Exterior rendering and painting
  • Fascia, soffit and gutter work

02 — EXTENSIONS

EXTENSION SCAFFOLDING

Rear and side extensions require a reliable scaffold that keeps pace with your builder. MAG Group works closely with contractors to ensure the scaffolding is always ready when the team needs it.

  • Single and double-storey extensions
  • Wrap-around extensions
  • Coordinated with your builder's programme
  • Safe vehicle and pedestrian access maintained

03 — CHIMNEY

CHIMNEY SCAFFOLDING

Chimney repairs require specialist access. Our chimney scaffold solutions provide a safe working platform at height, giving bricklayers and roofers the space they need to work properly.

  • Chimney pointing and repointing
  • Stack rebuilds and cowl replacements
  • Flashing repairs
  • Compliant with all working-at-height regulations

04 — NEW BUILD

NEW BUILD SCAFFOLDING

New build projects require a scaffolding partner who can keep pace with the construction programme. MAG Group provides full scaffold packages from groundworks to ridge — and adapts as the build progresses.

  • Full scaffold packages for new builds
  • Progressive lift and adaptation
  • Works alongside all main contractors
  • Surrey and London coverage

05 — CONVERSIONS

CONVERSION WORK

Loft and garage conversions demand precise, minimally invasive scaffolding. MAG Group has extensive experience on conversion projects, working carefully around occupied homes and tight access.

  • Loft conversions
  • Garage and outbuilding conversions
  • Works around occupied properties
  • Minimal disruption to neighbours

06 — COMMERCIAL

COMMERCIAL SCAFFOLDING

MAG Group handles large-scale commercial scaffold projects across Surrey and London. We work with developers and main contractors who need a reliable scaffolding partner who delivers on time and on budget.

  • Commercial buildings and offices
  • Large-scale refurbishments
  • Public realm and infrastructure works
  • Full method statements and RAMS provided
